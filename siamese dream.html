It was produced by the band's frontman Billy Corgan and renowned producer Butch Vig, who had previously worked on Nirvana's Nevermind. The album blends elements of alternative rock, grunge, and shoegaze, and features dense layers of guitar-driven sound, lush production, and emotionally charged lyrics.
